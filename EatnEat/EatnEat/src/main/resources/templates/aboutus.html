About Eat & Eat

Lunch is as delicious as COOKER with full of nutrients. Everything from ingredients, spices to packaging is taken care of by the Kitchen. We always:

  1. Use environmentally friendly paper boxes
  2. All dishes are used water through a quality filter
  3. Always prioritize natural and 'homemade' ingredients
  4. Verified origin
  5. Absolutely no chemicals or colorants
  6. Compliance with food hygiene and safety

Vision, mission, core values

Vision: To become the leading office lunch supplier in the city. Ho Chi Minh.

Mission: Improve the quality of food, the quality of life of Vietnamese people.

Core values: With the motto "DELICIOUS - CLEAN - SAFETY QUALITY"


GOOD is the most important factor that we bring to our customers.

  1. Chefs with many years of culinary experience, we bring DELICIOUS rice dishes to suit your taste and the BEST services to our customers.
  2. CLEAN: All dishes use water from a quality water purifier. We always prioritize natural and 'homemade' ingredients with clear origins
  3. SAFETY QUALITY: The kitchen uses environmentally friendly paper boxes. We are committed to absolutely no chemicals, colorants, no abuse of MSG and compliance with food hygiene and safety.
